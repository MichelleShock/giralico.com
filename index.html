Giralico

diviso per oceani - uniti da sangue

The surname is not a common one. Growing up in the United States, nobody knew how to pronounce my name. "Geraldo" "Geronimo" "Gear-al-eeko"... the list of botched pronounciations goes on.

As technology makes our world more accessible, I am connecting with more Giralico's around the globe. This site is a place for us to come together. Let's share our family experience and history.
